How to Interact:

Mouse: Press down with your mouse to make the circles move towards the center of the canvas.

Keys: Use the 1-0 keys (1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, or 0) on your keyboard to change the color of your circles.

Buttons: Click the buttons! When the "Add Custom Circle" button is clicked, you will be prompted for the size and color of a circle.When the "Add Random Circle" button is clicked, a random circle will be added to the canvas. By clicking on the "Delete All Circles", the canvas will be cleared.
