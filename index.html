How to Interact:

Mouse: Press down with your mouse to make the circles move towards your mouse.

Add Circles: When the "Add Custom Circle" button is clicked, you will be prompted for the size and color of a circle. When the"Add Random Circle" button is clicked, a random circle will be added to the canvas.

Delete Circles: By clicking on the "Delete All Circles", the canvas will be cleared. You can also press on the "R" key on your keyboard.

Keys: Use the 1-0 keys (1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, or 0) on your keyboard to change the color of your circles. You can also use the WASD keys to move the circles.
